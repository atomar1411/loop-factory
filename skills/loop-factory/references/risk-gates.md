# Risk Gates Reference

Stop and ask before changing:

- product semantics,
- money movement,
- legal or compliance behavior,
- security posture,
- safety or data-loss behavior,
- deployment config, secrets, or production systems,
- service boundaries,
- irreversible operations.

Use:

```text
Decision needed:
Why it matters:
Options:
Recommendation:
Risk if wrong:
Files or systems affected:
```
